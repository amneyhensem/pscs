About ZeroDay

ZeroDay is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering communities through education, nutrition, and clean water. Since 2015, we've impacted thousands of lives with the help of generous donors and passionate volunteers.

We believe every child deserves a chance to thrive. Join us in bridging hope and opportunity for those who need it most.
